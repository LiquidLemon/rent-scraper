How to create a Discord Webhook

  1. Go to your Discord server where you want to receive notifications
  2. Right-click on the channel where notifications should appear
  3. Select "Edit Channel"
  4. Go to the "Integrations" tab
  5. Click "Create Webhook"
  6. Give your webhook a name (e.g., "Rent Scraper")
  7. Click "Copy Webhook URL"
  8. Paste the URL in the field above
  9. Use the "Test Webhook" button to verify it works
🔐 Security Note:

Keep your webhook URLs private! Anyone with access to the URL can send messages to your Discord channel.
